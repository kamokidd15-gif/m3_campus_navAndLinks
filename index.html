Link Best Practices

  • Links should clearly describe what they do
  • Bad: Click here
  • Good: Contact us using our form

About Links

You will be learning about and creating six types of hyperlinks:

  • absolute
  • relative
  • fragment
  • email
  • telephone
  • relative with fragment

Absolute Hyperlinks

Absolute hyperlinks are used when links are needed to another website. For example, linking to asu.edu.

Relative Hyperlinks

Relative hyperlinks are used to navigate between pages within your own website, such as the contact page.

E-Mail Hyperlinks

You can email Hunter Williams using an email hyperlink.

Telephone Number Hyperlinks

Telephone hyperlinks can be used on supported devices. Example: (555) 555-5555

Fragment Hyperlinks

Fragment links jump to specific sections on the same page, such as back to the top.
